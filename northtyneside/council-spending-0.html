Spend data over £250: April 2014 - March 2015

Description:

Data for spend over £250 extracted from North Tyneside Council's Accounts Payable system and includes payments via BACS, Cheque and Purchase Cards for April 2014 onwards.

From April 2014, Credit notes will be reported with invoices, and purchase card transactions will be reported in separate files.

From December 2014, Purchase Card transactions are listed as Government Procurement Cards.
